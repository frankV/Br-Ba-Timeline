Timeline

The Breaking Bad Timeline was constructed with data collected from Sean Aranda's post "A Year in the Life of Walter White: A Breaking Bad Timeline" for data up to Season 5 part 1, Ray Rahman's "How Much Time Has Passed on Breaking Bad?" for seasons 1 through 3, and sheer love for the show.

Episode metadata is gathered from IMDB.

Avid fans already know that the show opens on Walt's 50th birhtday. By the time the fourth season starts, only a little more than seven months have actually passed. Walt's 51st birthday does not come around until Episode 4 of Season 5, "Fifty-One".

Right now we have a close approximation of the time passing in each episode up until the end of Season 5 part 1. We will continue to update and correct the timeline as we can.
